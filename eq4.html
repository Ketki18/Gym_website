Preacher Curl Bench

This bench is the perfect equipment for beginners to start their bicep training by making use of a curl bar. This equipment provides precise contraction, separating the elbow movement from the rest of the body.

Tips:

When you use the preacher curl bench,

  1. Sit with your triceps resting on the preacher bench pad and your underarms at the top of the edge.
  2. However, there are a few don'ts you need to avoid: Do not extend your arm too far; to get leverage and complete the curl, do not shift your body downwards toward the weight; do not use too much weight. This will lower the bar or dumbbell too far for down, making you lose control.
