Leg Curl/Leg Extension Machine

Work your glutes, hamstrings and quads like never before. The leg curl and leg extension dual purpose machine. This two in one machine not only works on multiple muscle groups but also helps in saving cost and space.

Tips:

Avoid these common mistakes when using the leg curl machine:

  1. Not placing the knees in line with the axis of the machine.
  2. Lifting the weight too fast to gain momentum.
  3. Extending your knees too far as you lower the weight.
